SAF MSP Calculator (Recruitment Bonus)

Minimum Service Period calculator for servicemen and servicewomen who draw a Recruitment Bonus (RB). MSP for Recruitment Bonus is calculated as three calendar years from the MSP start date.
Important Information on MSP for Recruitment Bonus

• Recruitment Bonus may be taken with surety or without surety.
• The earliest MSP start date is when the serviceman or servicewoman is first mustered into their vocation.
• MSP for RB is three calendar years from this date.
• Withdrawal of RB before MSP completion requires a surety.
• Withdrawal after MSP completion does not require a surety.

• This calculator applies only to MSP for Recruitment Bonus.
• A separate MSP may apply for Return of Investment (ROI) arising from training, which may be pegged to enlistment date or LOE where applicable.

• Recruitment Bonus and scholarships cannot be held concurrently. There is no two bites of the cherry.
• MSP for RB is independent of sponsorship or scholarship bonds.
• Servicewomen may be considered on a case by case basis.

• If unsure of MSP start date, please consult your Unit Manpower Officer or refer to your RB Deed, if available.
