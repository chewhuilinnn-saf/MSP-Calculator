SAF MSP Calculator

Minimum Service Period calculator for servicemen and servicewomen drawing recruitment bonus. MSP is three years from the date you are mustered to your vocation.

MSP is calculated as three calendar years from this date. Recruitment bonus surety is based on whether withdrawal happens before or after the MSP end date.

Enter your MSP start date above and tap Calculate MSP to view your MSP end date and surety requirement.
