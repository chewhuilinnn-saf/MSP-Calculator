SAF MSP Calculator

Minimum Service Period (MSP) calculator for servicemen and servicewomen drawing Recruitment Bonus (RB).

If you are unsure of your muster date, please consult your unit manpower officer or refer to PERNODES.

MSP for Recruitment Bonus (RB)
Recruitment Bonus Withdrawal Options

Option 1: Withdrawal with surety
Recruitment Bonus may be withdrawn before the MSP end date. A surety is required.

Option 2: Withdrawal without surety
Recruitment Bonus may be withdrawn on or after the MSP end date. No surety is required.
This calculator applies only to MSP for Recruitment Bonus.
• A separate MSP may apply for Return of Investment (ROI) arising from training, which may be pegged to enlistment date or LOE where applicable.

• MSP for RB is independent of sponsorship or scholarship bonds.
• Recruitment Bonus and scholarships cannot be held concurrently.
• Servicewomen may be considered on a case by case basis.

Please refer to your Recruitment Bonus Deed for authoritative terms and conditions.
