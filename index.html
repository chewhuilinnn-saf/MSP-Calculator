MSP for Recruitment Bonus (RB)

There are two ways to take Recruitment Bonus:

• With surety – withdrawal within MSP requires surety
• Without surety – withdrawal after MSP does not require surety

The earliest point MSP for RB can start is when a serviceman or servicewoman is mustered into their vocation. MSP for RB is calculated as three calendar years from this date.

If you are unsure of your mustering date, please refer to your Recruitment Bonus Deed or check with your Unit Manpower Officer or via PERNODES.
