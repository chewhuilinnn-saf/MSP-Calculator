SAF MSP Calculator

Minimum Service Period (MSP) calculator for servicemen and servicewomen drawing recruitment bonus. MSP is 3 calendar years from the MSP start date.

Example: date you are mustered to vocation or when recruitment bonus is first credited.
Surety is required if recruitment bonus is withdrawn before the MSP end date. No surety is required if withdrawn on or after the MSP end date.

Enter the MSP start date (and, if relevant, a planned withdrawal date), then tap Calculate MSP to see the MSP end date and whether a surety is required.
