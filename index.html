SAF MSP Calculator

Minimum Service Period calculator for servicemen and servicewomen drawing Recruitment Bonus (RB). MSP for RB is three calendar years from the MSP start date.
This is typically the date you are mustered into your vocation. If unsure, please refer to your Recruitment Bonus Deed or consult your Unit Manpower Officer.

Recruitment Bonus Options
Option 1: Take Recruitment Bonus with Surety
Recruitment Bonus may be drawn from the MSP start date.

If Recruitment Bonus is withdrawn before the MSP end date, a surety is required.
Option 2: Take Recruitment Bonus without Surety
Recruitment Bonus is drawn after completion of MSP.

No surety is required.
Additional Notes
1. MSP for RB is independent of sponsorship or scholarship bonds.

2. Servicewomen may be considered on a case by case basis.

Recruitment Bonus and scholarships cannot be held concurrently.
